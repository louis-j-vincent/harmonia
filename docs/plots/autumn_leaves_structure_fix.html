Autumn Leaves — chart-structure fix

The second A section was missing its final G-6, cascading a 1-bar left-shift through the entire song. Fixed 2026-07-14.

What this fix does: restores the correct metric position of every chord. Autumn Leaves is a 32-bar AABC form; each A ends on a double G-6. The chart had the double at the end of A1 (bars 6-7) but only a single G-6 at the end of A2, so bar 15 (Ah7, bridge) landed one bar early and everything downstream inherited the shift. Inserting one G-6 before each A->B boundary and renumbering makes both choruses clean 32-bar forms (0-31, 32-63). Because bars 0-7 are untouched, the refit tempo is identical (181 bpm); only downstream grid slots move by +1 bar (+1.326s each).

What it does NOT fix: the all-chord RMS residual of 39.0s is the drift between the raw DTW onset times and the constant 181-bpm grid (DTW times run to ~160s; the grid packs 64 bars into ~85s). That is a separate tempo/DTW issue — the constant head-tempo does not describe the whole recording. The play-along at /gt-playalong-corrected now uses the corrected grid; use it to hear where the DTW drift remains.
